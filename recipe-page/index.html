Pumpkin Pie S'mores Bars

If you love s’mores and pumpkin pie, these bars are going to be your new favorite fall treat. They’ve got the creamy, spiced pumpkin filling you’d expect, but layered with smooth chocolate ganache and gooey toasted marshmallows on top. It’s the best of both worlds in one cozy dessert.

PREP TIME: 40 MINTUES COOK TIME: 20 MINTUES

The Crust

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs (very fine)
  • 7 tablespoon unsalted butter, melted.
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar

Pumpkin Pie Filling

  • 1 can (15 oz) pumpkin puree
  • 1 can (14 oz) sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground giner
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
